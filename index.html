welcome to the sweet shop!

The Sweet Shop is nestled in a quaint neighborhood of North Los Altos and provides a welcoming atmosphere for family and friends to enjoy our Sweet Treats! Frozen Yogurt, Candy, Espresso Drinks, Baked Goods, Lunch and so much more. We are a community sweet store with Heart and the best part...all proceeds go to local schools!
